Recipe Hot-Dog

Makes 10 servings.

Ingredients:

Sauce:

  • Oil - 1 tablspoon
  • Sausages - 10 preboiled units
  • Tomate paste or tomato sauce - 500g
  • Onion - 1 chopped unit
  • Salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Hot dog bread - 10 pieces cut in half

Extras:

  1. Cheese (optional)
  2. Pickles (optional)
  3. Ketchup (optional)
  4. Mustard (optional)
  5. Mayonnaise (optional)
  6. Potato sticks (optional)

Preparing:

Pour the chopped onion after heating the required amount of oil in a large pan and saut√© until golden. Add the tomato paste (or tomato sauce), salt and leave on the heat for 5 minutes. After that, add the sausages and let everything cook for another 5 minutes.

Place a sausage on each bun and add sauce and extra ingredients to taste. Then, just enjoy your hot dog with the kids!
