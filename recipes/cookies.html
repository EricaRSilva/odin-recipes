Recipe Cookies

Makes 20 servings.

Ingredients:

  • Margaine- 150g
  • Sugar - 3/4 tea-cup
  • Brown suga - 1 tea-cup
  • Egg - 1 unit
  • Wheat flour - 1 and 3/4 tea-up
  • Baking chocolate - 1 tablespoon
  • Chopped chocolate - 300g
  • Cinnamon and vanilla - (optional)

Preparing:

A big bowl. In it, add the margarine, sugar and brown sugar and beat until you reach a uniform mixture. Add the egg and continue mixing. While still stirring, add the wheat flour, cinnamon and vanilla. Lastly, add the baking powder.

Then, with the batter already prepared, make small balls, using your hands. Place them in a greased pan (to prevent them from sticking) and flatten each of the balls made previously, making sure to leave space between them.

Place the pan in the preheated oven at 200 degrees Celsius, for around 20 minutes or until the edges of the cookies are darker. Remove from the oven, unmold after cooling and enjoy with the kids!
