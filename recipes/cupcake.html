Recipe Cupcake

Makes 15 servings.

Ingredients:

Batter:

  • Eggs - 2 units
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Wheat flour - 2 cups
  • Milk - 1 tea-cup
  • Melted butter - 2 tablespoons
  • Baking powder- 1/2 tablespoon

Cover:

  1. Chopped chocolate - 200g
  2. Table cream - 100g
  3. Chocolate sprinkles

Preparing:

In a large bowl, pour one ingredient at a time and stir everything until you create a uniform mixture. Place it in molds and bake at medium temperature for 30 minutes.

While the batter is baking, melt the chopped chocolate in a bain-marie or in the microwave (be careful not to burn it!) and mix with the table cream. Then, put it in the fridge until it hardens, reaching an ointment point. To spread the frosting over the cupcakes, use a piping tip or spoon. Add chocolate sprinkles to taste.
